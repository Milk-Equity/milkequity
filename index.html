Milk Equity

We make money, simple as that.

Milk Equity was founded in 2021 with the intention of giving its members a place to learn and practice valuable real-world skills around public security investment. As a Milk Equity member, you have the opportunity to pitch investments, get peer feedback, and practice equity valuation skills. As a self-governed partnership, Milk Equity seeks to maximize returns against our market benchmark, the S&P500. All members have contributed investment capital, and all decisions are made with the best interest of all members. While real money is at stake, all members recognize mistakes may be made and that mistakes are part of the learning process.
